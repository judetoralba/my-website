About Us

The TakeoutPH Marketing Team, Nicola Andrea Encallado, Miguela Louise Alcantara, and Alice Hamase, was created to connect TakeoutPH with its student community. As a small stall, we assembled a team to boost visibility and emphasize our purpose: providing students with accessible, comforting meals. TakeoutPH began in a small Dapitan space in front of a busy student gate, inspired by students who needed fast, affordable, and reliable food.

How Takeout Started: Your Go-To Spot in Dapitan

“Since we already had a space in Dapitan, we wanted to make something useful out of it. Being right in front of the building made it convenient for students who are always in a rush. That’s when we thought of creating a go-to food and beverage spot that offers accessibility and comfort—good food, fast and convenient for students who need it most.” — Alice Hamase, TakeoutPH
